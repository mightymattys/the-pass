## Acceptance Evidence

- Engine-neutral protocols define strategy, feature, cost, fill, portfolio, risk, and
  result boundaries.
- The NumPy/pandas screen runner evaluates every preregistered variant and stores every
  result.
- The deterministic event simulator uses the same `CanonicalEvent` and decision contract
  intended for paper replay.
- Market fills walk opposing depth and reject unavailable remainder. Limit fills require
  subsequent trade/book evidence and apply queue plus adverse-selection haircuts.
- Bar fills require a subsequent bar and adverse slippage. Midpoint fills are explicitly
  diagnostic and cannot support promotion.
- Portfolio accounting separates cash, collateral, realized/unrealized PnL, fees, funding,
  borrow, roll, opportunity cost, rejected and missed evidence; conservation is checked
  after every fill and mark.
- Six public synthetic packages cover buy-and-hold, seeded random, Donchian momentum,
  volatility-filtered mean reversion, futures trend, and prediction complement diagnostics.
- Every package includes StrategySpec, data manifest, quality report, receipt, metrics,
  cost waterfall, verdict, preregistered search space, all screen results, JSON detail,
  Markdown, static HTML, and a verifiable v2 run ledger.
